Lillia is my most favorite jungler in the game. She has a simple interesting kit that plays around constant damage, not burst. But if you want to, you can build burst. But mostly she is tank-killer.
Most fun part of her kit is q's passive, that makes her so fast. Because of Lillia, i started to dodge skillshots much better.
I like her teamfight potential with ultimate that jungler must have. Also she is cute deer! Wonderful champion to play!

Xin Zhao is one of the best junglers almost every season. That's why i tried play Xin. But his simple gameplay and similliarity to Jax made me having fun playing him. He is bruiser too and can play duels well.
When Jax was bad in jungle, Xin became really good champ for me, and now he still is.
Also i like his lore, he has one of the best lore, which was really polished by Season 15.
